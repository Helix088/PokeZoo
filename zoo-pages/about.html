About Us

Who are we?

We are a nonprofit organization. The PokéZoo is home to the world's first online Pokémon Zoo where you can view over a 1000 species of Pokémon from the comfort of your own home! We have done this for the enjoyment of all and to help foster in a new age of zoos that do not hold any animals (and Pokémon) in captivity. We believe animals (and Pokémon) should remain where they are in their own environments to live long and happy lives. We love animals (and Pokémon) and are advocates for them in anyway that we can be.

What do we do?

We are in the field of education and adventure and we do this by helping people become aware of their local and global environment. We help by taking in donation from generous donors and give all of that money to animals conservation agencies. We accomplish this goal by creating this new virtual zoo to help show people that they can enjoy the same aspects of the zoo, and not have to goal to a physical zoo. We work with animal conservation agencies to provide valuable information to help raise awareness that animals are in need of our help.
